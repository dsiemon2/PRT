## 5. How To Evaluate a Dropshipper + API For Your Stack

When you contact or test a supplier/platform, you’ll want answers to:

1. **API & Access**
   - Is there a **public REST API** or only CSV/XML feeds?
   - What’s covered: products, inventory, pricing, orders, tracking?
   - Are there **rate limits** or minimum monthly order requirements?

2. **Catalog Fit**
   - For **Western Wear**: boots, hats, denim, fringe jackets, rodeo apparel, western jewelry.
   - For **Australian Wear**: outback hats, rugged workwear, warm‑weather apparel, AU‑friendly brands.
   - For **Survival Gear**: kits, water filters, fire‑starters, first aid, multitools, non‑restricted defensive gear.

3. **Logistics**
   - Where are warehouses (US, AU, EU, CN)?
   - Average shipping times and carriers to your target markets (US & AU are your big ones).
   - Return policy and defective product handling.

4. **Data Quality**
   - Image resolution and count per product.
   - Consistent sizing charts for apparel.
   - Clear tags for materials, compliance, and restrictions.

5. **Business Terms**
   - Subscription fees vs per‑order fees.
   - MAP (minimum advertised price) rules for branded western gear.
   - Whether you can **white‑label / private label** (useful for your own brand).

## 6. Quick Next‑Steps Checklist

1. **Pick your automation hub** (Doba, AppScenic, Inventory Source, Spark Shipping, Flxpoint).  
2. **Filter for each niche**:
   - Western wear apparel & accessories  
   - AU‑based suppliers & “outback”/rugged styles  
   - Survival / tactical / camping gear  
3. **Request API docs & sandbox keys** from each platform you plan to integrate directly.  
4. **Stand up a test store** (Shopify or WooCommerce) and connect:
   - 1 western‑friendly catalog  
   - 1 AU‑based catalog  
   - 1 survival/outdoor catalog  
5. **Test end‑to‑end**: product import → price/inventory sync → test orders → tracking callbacks.  
6. Once stable, **narrow down to 2–4 core suppliers/platforms** that cover your niches with reliable shipping and good margin.
